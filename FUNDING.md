# RepoGround funding principles

RepoGround is free to use, study, modify, and redistribute under the
Apache License, Version 2.0. Payment is not required for access to the
software or its source code.

Funding, if introduced, should follow these principles:

- contributions are voluntary donations or transparent sponsorships;
- donors receive no ownership of the community, project name, roadmap, or
  maintainer decisions merely because they donated;
- no essential feature is intentionally withheld to pressure payment;
- material income and project spending should be reported transparently;
- fundraising under the official RepoGround name must follow
  `TRADEMARK_POLICY.md` and be attributable to the official project;
- a future non-profit steward is preferred over permanent personal control
  when the community is mature enough to sustain it.

No donation channel is currently declared by this document. It records the
conditions that any future channel should satisfy.
